**Abra Chan Community Guidelines**  
*Last updated: June 2026*  
*Version: 1.1*  
*Repository: https://github.com/abra-chan/VTuber-policy-docs*

### Welcome to the Das Hünerhaus 🐔
This is a **weird, positive, high-signal** community. We talk about VTubing, gaming, magic, life, memes, and whatever else comes up — without turning every stream into a culture war battlefield or an identity parade.

**Core Philosophy**
- Be excellent to each other.
- Signal > noise.
- No echo chamber, but also no constant grandstanding.
- We value honesty and pattern recognition over enforced politeness.
- Keep the vibe fun and watchable for everyone.

### The Actual Rules

#### 1. Be Respectful & Civil
- No targeted harassment, doxxing, witch-hunting, or personal attacks on other community members.
- Disagreement is fine. Turning someone into your personal lolcow is not.
- Treat people as individuals, not avatars for their identity group.

#### 2. Keep Chat On-Topic
- During stream: focus on the game, the stream, VTubing, or related topics.
- Off-topic is okay in moderation — just don’t derail constantly.

#### 3. No Spam or Unauthorized Self-Promotion
- No random links, copypastas, or advertising unless I ask.
- No DM spam to other members.

#### 4. Seiso / No NSFW
- Keep it PG-13. No explicit sexual content, extreme gore, or porn links.
- Light banter is usually fine. Being gross isn’t.

#### 5. No Virtue Signaling or Off-Topic Politics
- Don’t make every conversation about your identity, oppression, or political team.
- Unrelated political/SJW lectures or bait will be moderated so the stream stays enjoyable.
- Topical, good-faith discussion is welcome when it fits naturally and stays civil.
- Mods prioritize stream quality over debate club.

#### 6. No Targeted Hate or Harassment
We do **not** run a “safe space.” Honest talk, pattern recognition, and criticism of ideas/ideologies (including gender, race, politics, etc.) are allowed.

What **is** not allowed:
- Targeted harassment or slurs directed at specific individuals in chat.
- Deliberately trying to get someone to quit or have a meltdown for entertainment (lolcow hunting).
- Spamming identity politics or using chat as your personal activism platform.
- Malicious doxxing or real-world harm attempts.

You can criticize modern feminism, transgender ideology, DEI, immigration patterns, crime statistics, or whatever else — **as long as** it stays at the level of ideas and doesn’t become repeated personal attacks on other chatters. Cool trans/gay/black/brown/indigenous/whatever people who just want to vibe and talk about games are 100% welcome. People who make everything about their identity or demand special treatment are not.

Platforms still have their own rules. Extreme stuff can still get us in trouble, so use some common sense.

#### 7. Follow Platform Rules + VTuber Etiquette
- Respect Twitch, Kick, YouTube, Discord, etc. ToS.
- No stream sniping, malicious raiding, or backseat modding.
- Credit creators when sharing art/clips.

#### 8. Don’t Make Abra Look Dumb
- Report technical issues (chroma key disasters, dead audio, etc.) immediately.

### Consequences
Progressive system (mods have discretion):

| Severity | First | Repeat |
|----------|-------|--------|
| Minor (spam, off-topic) | Warning | Timeout |
| Medium (grandstanding, targeted jabs) | Timeout | Longer ban |
| Severe (harassment, real harm) | Ban | Permanent |

### Reporting & Appeals
- DM mods or use #mod-mail. You can appeal to me directly if you think a mod was unfair.
- Twitch unban requests will be reviewed on-stream monthly for conent.

### Changes & Transparency
This document is public in git. Suggest edits via issues or Discord feedback. I have final say, but I actually want your input as we grow.

**Final Note**  
Let’s keep this place weird, honest, and fun. You don’t have to agree with me or anyone else on everything — just don’t make it exhausting for everyone else.  

Now let’s go make some chaos 🐔
