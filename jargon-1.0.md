# Das Hünerhaus Jargon File

**Version 1.0** — Last updated: June 2026

This is the living lexicon of **Das Hünerhaus**. Like the old hacker Jargon File, it records the special language, memes, and shared understanding that binds our weird little chicken cult together. 

If you hear something you don't understand, look it up here. If it's missing and should be here, suggest it in `#community-feedback`.

---

**ABRASAX**  
1. prop. n. The sacred word of Das Hünerhaus. A powerful incantation and focus for meditation. Members are instructed to meditate upon it for seven minutes after completing the Harrowing.  
2. Ancient Gnostic term (also spelled Abraxas) for the Great Archon, ruler of 365 heavens. The Greek letters of the name sum numerically to 365, linking it to the solar year and cosmic order.  
3. Frequently depicted on ancient magical gems with a rooster’s head (symbol of vigilance and the dawn), a human body, and serpents for legs — representing the union of opposites and hidden forces behind creation.
4. In Hünerhaus usage: a call to transcendence, alignment, and controlled chaos. Invoked when something particularly cursed or blessed occurs on stream.
5. Connected to the 18th-century French grimoire *The Black Pullet* (*La Poule Noire* / *La Poule aux Œufs d’Or*), which features talismanic magic and the motif of the magical hen — a thematic bridge to our poultry mysticism.
   See also: *Harrowing*, *poule*, *coq*, *The Black Pullet*.

**BASED DEPARTMENT**  
1. n. The collective of people who are unapologetically honest and refuse to bend to social pressure.  
2. A playful way to praise someone for a based take. "Based Department, reporting in."  
3. The opposite of virtue-signaling echo chamber behavior. See also: *high-signal*.

**CHEEPS**  
1. n. A newly hatched member of the community. The default starting role before undergoing the Harrowing.  
2. A term of endearment for fresh arrivals. "Look at all these new cheeps in the yard tonight."  
3. See also: *Harrowing*, *Chook*.

**CHOOK**  
1. n. A term of endearment for a member of the community. "Alright you beautiful chooks, let's get weird."  
2. n. Any loyal viewer or participant in the Hünerhaus, regardless of species.  
3. From Australian English "chook" (chicken). See also: *Hühner*, *f'lahk*.

**COQ**  
1. n. Rooster (French).
2. Reference to *Coq au Vin*, a classic French dish with roots in ancient Gaul. Legends also link the rooster to Julius Caesar and Gallic symbolism.
3. Used in the main announcements channel **#coq-a-doodle**.

**EN DIRECT**  
1. adj. / adv. “Live” or “in real time” (French).
2. Implies unfiltered authenticity, raw presence, and stream-of-consciousness energy.
3. Name of the main subscriber voice channel: **poules-en-direct** — the place for live collabs, gaming, and real-time interaction during streams.

**ETERNAL-SPRING**  
1. n. The private topical channel for Faith, Philosophy, Cults & Mystery Schools.  
2. A place where Saturn-aligned minds go to get even weirder.

**F'LAHK**  
1. n. The flock; the collective body of the community. "The f'lahk is strong tonight."  
2. From *f'lah* in *The Elder Scrolls III: Morrowind*, meaning "brother" or "bro".  
   Used ironically or affectionately among night owls and chaos enjoyers.

**GREAT HOUSE**  
1. n. One of the seven noble chicken houses: Silkie, Leghorn, Orpington, Easter, Giant, Comet, Rock.
2. Inspired by the Great Houses of Morrowind. Each has its own personality, private channel, and role color. Members are assigned to a Great House following their initiation.

**GREAT HOUSE POINTS**  
1. n. The communal currency of the Hünerhaus. Earned through active participation, memes, raids, and positive contributions.  
2. Lost through rule-breaking, toxicity, or excessive off-topic posting.  
3. Points contribute to your house’s standing in the Pecking Order.  
   See also: *Pecking Order*.

**HARROWING**  
1. n. The sacred initiation rite in which a Cheep takes the Sorting Quiz to be assigned to one of the seven Great Houses and earn full Chook status.  
2. Not all who enter the Harrowing emerge as Chooks — some are returned to the egg. A running inside joke.  
3. From the ancient Mage trial in *Dragon Age: Origins*. See also: *Great House*, *Cheeps*.

**HAUS**  
1. n. House (German).  
2. In context: a place of belonging, a roost, or a Great House.
   "I just got sorted into House Barred-Rock, send help!"

**HÜHNER**  
1. n. Chicken (German). The base species of our digital coop.  
2. Often used as a playful plural or collective. "All my Hühner in the chat tonight!"

**HÜHNERHAUS**  
1. n. The Chicken House. The official name of our community and server.  
2. A place of controlled chaos, late-night streams, and high-signal conversation.  
   See also: *Das Hünerhaus*, *the Coop*.

**LUSTRATE**  
1. v. To purify or cleanse.  
2. The official fan-art tag on X/Twitter (#abraLustrate). Used when members create wholesome or high-quality art of Abra Channel.  
3. "That piece really lustrated the stream tonight."

**PECKING ORDER**  
1. n. The weekly leaderboard showing which Great House is currently dominant based on accumulated points.  
2. "Barred-Rocks taking the top spot in the Pecking Order again — typical."

**POULE**  
1. n. Chicken (French).  
2. Reference to the 18th-century grimoire *The Black Pullet* (*La Poule aux Œufs d’Or*), a legendary text of occult magic and hidden knowledge.  
3. The playful root of our “Poules LIVE” subscriber area.  
   See also: *poules*, *coq*, *Abrasax*.

**POULES**  
1. n. Hens (French).  
2. Colloquial term for girls/women — can be playful, affectionate, or slightly pejorative depending on context.  
3. The collective name for subscribers and active community members in the “Poules LIVE” area.

**SEISO**  
1. adj. Clean, wholesome, or family-friendly. "Keep it seiso in main chat."  
2. The opposite of gooning or excessive degeneracy.  
3. From Japanese "seiso" (clean/pure). Used both seriously and ironically in VTuber/streamer culture.  
   See also: *PG-13*, *no NSFW*.

**SORTING**  
1. v. The act of taking the **Harrowing** (the initiation quiz) and being assigned to one of the seven Great Houses.  
2. "Yay! I just got sorted into Great House Bantam-Silkies! Silkie HYPE~"  
3. See also: *Harrowing*, *Cheeps*, *Great House*, *Pecking Order*.

**STARDUST**  
1. n. Channel Points on Twitch. Used for redeems and community rewards.  
2. "Drop some Stardust on the chicken dance redeem."
